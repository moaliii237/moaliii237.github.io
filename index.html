Root segmentation research

Solo

A controlled study of how the way you cut an image into patches changes both segmentation quality and the actual measurement of root length. I trained a ResUNet across ten configurations and tested the results with proper statistics.

Larger patches cut measurement error from 24% to 14%, and the best Dice score was not the most accurate at measuring length.
